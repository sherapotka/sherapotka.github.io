Fairness • Bias • Privacy in AI

I teach Data Models & Algorithms and study fairness in recommenders, bias in LLMs, and privacy‑preserving NLP. I build methods that elevate minority visibility, measure/mitigate embedding bias, and sanitize text with differential privacy.

Research Focus

Fairness in Social Recommenders

Improving minority visibility and mitigating popularity bias while preserving utility.

Bias in Large Language Models

Measuring gender & race bias in embeddings and contextual models (e.g., SC‑WEAT).

Privacy in NLP

Differentially private text sanitization that maintains semantic coherence (e.g., CluSanT).
