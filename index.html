Researcher & Instructor

I’m a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at the University of Victoria. My work focuses on fairness in recommender systems, bias in large language models, and privacy in natural language processing. I design embedding-based methods that make AI systems more inclusive, accountable, and privacy-preserving. My long-term goal is to build a unified framework of “ethical embeddings” for responsible AI in law, education, and healthcare.

Research Focus

Fairness in Social Recommenders

Minority visibility and popularity-bias mitigation with minimal utility loss.

Bias in Large Language Models

Measuring gender & race bias in embeddings and contextual models (e.g., SC-WEAT).

Privacy in NLP

Differentially private text sanitization with semantic fidelity (e.g., CluSanT).
