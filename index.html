Shera Potka

Researcher & Instructor

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Victoria. My work focuses on fairness in recommender systems, bias in large language models, and privacy in NLP. I design embedding-based methods that make AI systems more inclusive, accountable, and privacy-preserving.

Publications Download CV

Research Focus

Fairness in Social Recommenders

Minority visibility and popularity-bias mitigation with minimal utility loss.

Bias in Large Language Models

Measuring gender & race bias in embeddings and contextual models (e.g., SC-WEAT).

Privacy in NLP

Differentially private text sanitization with semantic fidelity (e.g., CluSanT).
